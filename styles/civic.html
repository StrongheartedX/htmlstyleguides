Chapter IX

Design Principles

Core values that guide every design decision in the Civic system.

Trustworthy, Not Militant

Navy and gold evoke military heritage subtly without aggressive patriotic imagery. The aesthetic is professional yet warm.

Clarity Over Decoration

Information hierarchy is paramount. Every element serves a purpose in helping veterans find resources quickly.

Accessible by Default

18px base font, high contrast ratios, visible focus states, and generous spacing ensure the interface works for everyone.

Warm & Approachable

Soft shadows, rounded corners, and lift animations create a tactile, inviting experience that respects cognitive load.
