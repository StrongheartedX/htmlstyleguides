09

Design Principles

Embrace Imperfection

Misregistration, ink bleed, and grain are features, not bugs. The beauty of risograph lies in its happy accidents and organic inconsistencies.

Layer with Purpose

Each color is a separate pass through the machine. Design with overprinting in mind, letting colors mix and create new tones where they overlap.

Limited Palette

Work within constraints. A limited color palette forces creativity and creates cohesion. Two or three spot colors can achieve remarkable range.

DIY Spirit

Risograph emerged from the independent publishing scene. Maintain that grassroots, accessible, punk-inspired energy in every design decision.
