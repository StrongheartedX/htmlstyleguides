The Art of Infinite Pattern

Islamic geometric art emerged from a profound understanding that mathematics and spirituality are intertwined. The repetition of patterns to infinity reflects the boundless nature of God, while the precision of the geometry demonstrates the order underlying creation. From the Alhambra in Spain to the mosques of Morocco, these patterns have adorned the most sacred spaces for over a thousand years.
