09

Design Principles

The philosophy behind the embroidery aesthetic.

Grid Alignment

Like Aida cloth with its counted squares, maintain strict grid alignment. Every element should snap to the pixel grid for that authentic cross-stitch precision.

Texture & Depth

Layer fabric textures and thread shadows to create tactile warmth. The raised quality of stitches should feel dimensional, not flat.

Handmade Warmth

Embrace the cozy, nostalgic feeling of handcrafted items. Each interface should feel like it was made with love, patience, and care.

Color Harmony

Draw from traditional embroidery floss palettes. Vibrant thread colors pop against natural fabric backgrounds, creating cheerful contrast.

Embroidery Design System

Crafted with care, one stitch at a time. A tribute to the timeless art of needlework and the joy of handmade creation.
