09

Design Principles

The core values behind this cozy game aesthetic, distilled from the warmest farming sims and life simulators.

01

Warmth First

Every color, shadow, and border should radiate warmth. No harsh edges, no cold grays. Think sunlit afternoons and firelit evenings.

02

Chunky & Tactile

Thick borders, deep shadows, and rounded corners make every element feel like a physical object you could reach out and touch.

03

Pixel Nostalgia

Blend pixel art sensibilities with modern comfort. VT323 stats, grid-aligned spacing, and subtle pixel textures honor retro roots without feeling dated.

04

Joyful Feedback

Buttons bounce, cards lift, and focus rings glow. Every interaction should feel rewarding, like the satisfying "ding" of a level-up.
