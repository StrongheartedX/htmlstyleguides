C09

Design Principles

Core principles derived from PCB design practices and hardware engineering standards.

01

Grid Alignment

Every element snaps to a standardized grid (0.1" pitch). Precise alignment enables clean trace routing and professional component placement.

02

Signal Integrity

Clear visual hierarchy reduces noise. Important information gets thicker traces (gold, copper). Secondary content uses thinner lines (silkscreen).

03

Functional Labeling

Every component is clearly designated. Silkscreen labels identify function. Reference designators follow IPC standards (R, C, U, D, LED).

04

Material Authenticity

Colors and textures reflect real PCB materials: FR-4 green substrate, copper metallization, silver solder, white silkscreen. Hardware-accurate design.
