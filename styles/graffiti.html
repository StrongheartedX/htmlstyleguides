Typography

Three typefaces for three voices: tags, throw-ups, and body text on the wall.

Display / Tags — Rubik Spray Paint
Spray Paint Tags

Font: Rubik Spray Paint · Use for hero text, splash displays, large decorative titles

Headings / Throw-ups — Permanent Marker
Quick Marker Headings

Font: Permanent Marker · Use for section headings, labels, UI emphasis

Body / Utility — Space Grotesk
Space Grotesk brings clean geometric readability to the chaos. Used for body copy, metadata, form fields, and anywhere legibility matters on the wall. Available in weights 400, 500, and 700.
Bold weight for emphasis and strong labels. The structured backbone behind every wild piece.

06

Cards / Panels

Containers for content on the wall. Wheat-pasted posters, concrete slabs, tagged panels, and gallery frames.

Wheat-Paste Poster

Aged paper background with torn edges, like a poster slapped on the wall weeks ago. The rain has warped the corners but the message still stands. Perfect for announcements and featured content.

Concrete Panel

A slab of digital concrete with chrome trim. Good for code blocks, data tables, or anything that needs a hard, structured surface.

07

Alerts / Notifications

Messages slapped on the wall. Caution tape warnings, spray-dripped errors, neon success, and chrome info panels.

⚠
Warning: Wet Paint

This section is still being painted. Watch your step and do not lean on the wall. The clear coat has not been applied yet.

❌
Danger: Busted

Security spotted. Time to pack the cans and bounce. This wall is too hot right now. Come back another night.

✔
Success: Piece Completed

The mural is finished. Colors are clean, outlines are crisp, and the fill is even. Time to sign it and photograph the work.

ℹ
Info: Wall Status

This wall is a legal spot. Permission granted by the property owner. Paint freely but respect the existing work and neighboring pieces.
