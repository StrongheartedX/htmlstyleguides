Design Principles

Four guiding ideas that keep every Tropical interface feeling warm, bold, and authentically paradise-inspired.

Guidelines
01

Vibrant, Not Garish

Use saturated color with intention. Bold coral for primary actions, vivid lagoon for accents, but always balanced with warm neutrals and generous whitespace.

02

Warm Energy

Every interaction should feel inviting. Sunset gradients, bouncy hover effects, and rounded shapes create an atmosphere of relaxed enthusiasm.

03

Natural Depth

Layer surfaces like a tropical landscape: sand in the back, palms in the middle, vivid flowers up front. Use shadows and elevation to create rich spatial hierarchy.

04

Lush Detail

Small touches make the experience memorable. Gradient accents on headers, colored status dots, leaf patterns in backgrounds -- the paradise is in the details.
