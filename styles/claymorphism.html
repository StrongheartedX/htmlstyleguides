The Clay Effect

Claymorphism combines the soft, organic feel of neumorphism with vibrant pastel colors. The result is a playful, tactile interface that looks like it was shaped from colorful modeling clay. Elements appear to float slightly above the surface while maintaining a soft, approachable aesthetic.
