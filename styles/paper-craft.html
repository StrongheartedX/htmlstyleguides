09

Design Principles

The philosophy behind paper craft aesthetics.

Layered Depth

Stack elements like sheets of paper to create natural depth. Use shadows to show elevation and relationship between components.

Embrace Imperfection

Slight rotations, torn edges, and dog-eared corners add character. Perfect alignment can feel cold; intentional imperfection feels human.

Tactile Textures

Simulate the feel of different paper stocks through subtle gradients, patterns, and shadows. Users should almost feel the texture.

Handmade Warmth

Combine handwritten fonts with crafted decorations. The goal is to evoke the care and attention of something made by hand.
