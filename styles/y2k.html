09

Design Principles

The core values that define Y2K aesthetic.

+

Tech Optimism

Embrace the belief that technology makes everything better. The future is bright, connected, and full of possibility. Design should feel hopeful and forward-looking.

~

Playful Futurism

The future doesn't have to be cold and sterile. Y2K celebrates color, curves, and personality. Technology should be fun, friendly, and accessible to everyone.

Key Characteristics

Translucency

Glass-like surfaces, frosted effects, layers that reveal depth.

Glossy Surfaces

Gel buttons, chrome effects, shiny highlights that catch light.

Rounded Forms

Bubble shapes, soft corners, organic curves everywhere.

Bold Colors

iMac palette, neon accents, cyber gradients.

Light Effects

Lens flares, starbursts, glowing elements.

Digital Motifs

Binary, circuits, @ symbols, tech iconography.
